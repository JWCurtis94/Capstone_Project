FIA Rules

Welcome to the official FIA page for Wheel2Wheel. Below, you'll find the official rules that all participants must adhere to during races. Please read them carefully before each event.

  • Rule 1: No intentional contact with other drivers.
  • Rule 2: Respect all blue flags.
  • Rule 3: Exceeding track limits will result in penalties.
  • Rule 4: All drivers must attend the drivers' briefing before the race.
  • Rule 5: FIA decisions are final and must be respected.
